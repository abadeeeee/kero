About Me

I am a dedicated and passionate English teacher with experience in both traditional and online educational settings. Currently, I work as an English Teacher at Smart City International School (SCIS) in New Asyut, Egypt, where I have been shaping young minds since September 2022. In addition to my role at SCIS, I served as an English Instructor at English Capsules, an online teaching platform, from July to October 2024. This experience allowed me to develop strong digital teaching skills and adapt to remote learning environments.

Before joining SCIS, I worked as an English Teacher at Futures Language School in New Asyut (September 2021 – September 2022), where I gained valuable classroom management and curriculum delivery experience. I am committed to fostering a love for the English language in my students through engaging lessons, innovative teaching methods, and a student-centered approach. My goal is to inspire confidence and proficiency in English while adapting to diverse learning needs.
